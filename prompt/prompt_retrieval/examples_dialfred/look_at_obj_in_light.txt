High Level Goal: Examine a CD under a lamp.
Low Level Goal:
(1) Walk to the desk in front of you and turn left towards the shelf above it ",
(2) Pick up the CD that is laying on the shelf",
(3) Turn around and walk to the lamp sitting on the stand across the room",
(4) Turn on the lamp that is on top of the stand"
Python script:
target_cd = InteractionObject("CD", landmark = "Shelf")
target_cd.go_to()
target_cd.pickup()
target_lamp = InteractionObject("DeskLamp", landmark = "Cabinet")
target_lamp.go_to()
target_lamp.toggle_on()
