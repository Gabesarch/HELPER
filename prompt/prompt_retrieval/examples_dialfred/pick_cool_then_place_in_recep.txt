High Level Goal: Set a chilled bottle of wine on the table.
Low Level Goal:
(1) Go across the room to the table.
(2) Pick up the wine bottle on the table.
(3) Go across the room to the fridge.
(4) Chill the wine in the fridge.
(5) Turn right and go to the table on the left past the fridge.
(6) Set the chilled wine down on the front right corner of the table.
Python script:
target_winebottle = InteractionObject("WineBottle", landmark = "DiningTable")
target_winebottle.go_to()
target_winebottle.pickup()
target_fridge = InteractionObject("Fridge")
target_fridge.go_to()
target_fridge.open()
target_winebottle.place(target_fridge)
target_fridge.close()
target_fridge.open()
target_winebottle.go_to()
target_winebottle.pickup()
target_diningtable = InteractionObject("DiningTable")
target_diningtable.go_to()
target_winebottle.place(target_diningtable)
