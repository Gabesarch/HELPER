High Level Goal: Put a cooked/heated up slice of tomato in a sink.
Low Level Goal:
(1) Go forward all the way, turn right, go forward all the way to the counter with the toaster.
(2) Take the knife on the counter from behind the plate.
(3) Stay at the counter.
(4) Cut the tomato on the counter into slices.
(5) Turn to the left.
(6) Put the knife on the counter to the right of the tomato.
(7) Turn to the right.
(8) Take a slice of tomato from the counter.
(9) Turn around, go forward, turn right to the oven.
(10) Heat/cook the tomato in the microwave about the oven then take it out.
(11) Turn around, go forward, turn left at the sink, go to the sink.
(12) Put the tomato on the right side of the sink.
Python script:
target_knife = InteractionObject("Knife", landmark = "CounterTop")
target_knife.pickup()
target_tomato = InteractionObject("Tomato", landmark = "CounterTop")
target_tomato.go_to()
target_tomato.slice()
target_countertop = InteractionObject("CounterTop")
target_knife.place(target_countertop) # free up hand
target_tomatosliced = InteractionObject("TomatoSliced", landmark = "CounterTop")
target_tomatosliced.go_to()
target_tomatosliced.pickup()
target_microwave = InteractionObject("Microwave")
target_microwave.go_to()
target_microwave.open() # open microwave before placing
target_tomatosliced.place(target_microwave) 
target_microwave.close() # close microwave before toggle on
target_microwave.toggle_on() # toggle on to heat up
target_microwave.toggle_off() # Important! toggle off to end heating. Do not try to open microwave without toggle off!
target_microwave.open() # open microwave before picking
target_tomatosliced.pickup()
target_sink = InteractionObject("Sink")
target_sink.go_to()
target_tomatosliced.place(target_sink)
