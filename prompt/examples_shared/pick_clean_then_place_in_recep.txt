High Level Goal: Place a washed tomato slice in the microwave.
Low Level Goal:
(1) Turn right, move to the corner with the brown shelving unit, turn right again and stop between the window and counter, facing the tomatoes.
(2) Pick up the tomato closest to you from the counter.
(3) Carry the tomato to the kitchen sink.
(4) Place the tomato in the kitchen sink.
(5) Pick up the knife from the kitchen sink.
(6) Slice the top off the tomato in the sink.
(7) Place the knife in the sink.
(8) Pick up the slice you just cut from the tomato in the sink.
(9) Place the slice of tomato in the sink, turn the water on, and wash it.
(10) Remove the freshly washed tomato slice from the sink.
(11) Place the tomato slice in the microwave and shut the door.
Python script:
target_tomato = InteractionObject("Tomato", landmark = "Window", attributes = ["clean"])
target_tomato.go_to()
target_tomato.pickup()
target_sinkbasin = InteractionObject("SinkBasin" landmark = "Sink")
target_tomato.place(target_sinkbasin)
target_knife = InteractionObject("Knife", landmark = "Sink")
target_knife.pickup()
target_tomato.go_to()
target_tomato.slice()
target_knife.place(target_sinkbasin)
target_tomatosliced = InteractionObject("TomatoSliced", landmark = "SinkBasin")
target_tomatosliced.go_to()
target_tomatosliced.pickup()
target_tomatosliced.clean()
target_microwave = InteractionObject("Microwave")
target_microwave.go_to()
target_microwave.open()
target_tomatosliced.place(target_microwave)
target_microwave.close()
